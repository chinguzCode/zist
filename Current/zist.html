OUR STORY,behind what we do...

Zimbabwe Institute of Strategic Thinking (ZIST) is for Zimbabwe in which strategists, critical minds, researchers, policy makers, civic society, academics and individuals will participate more in strategy, critical thinking, position papers, policy dialogue, research, capacity development and institutional building, and are free to exercise their participation despite race, gender, sex, colour using various platforms of trainings different skills, holding public meetings and other forums that maybe advancement of their economic status. Zimbabwe Institute of Strategic Thinking (ZIST) strives to be a people centered, neutral and effective platform for Zimbabweans through strategic thinking, creating position papers, policy dialogue meetings, public meetings, trainings, and awareness in skills rising through the provision of interaction with different stakeholders throughout the whole world.
